Google Analytics Customer Revenue Prediction

Predicted transaction revenue of Google Merchandise Store.

View on GitHub

About the Project

The main objective is to predict transaction revenue for the following 60 days and to predict transaction revenue based on each customer visiting Google Store. The research question was broken down into three parts:

  1. Forecast daily transaction revenue by using a time series model
  2. Classify whether a customer is going to purchase some products
  3. Predict the total revenue a customer is going to make

Methodology

  • Fitted seasonal ARIMA model to capture weekly pattern
  • Performed nested cross-validation to avoid overfitting for time series model
  • Applied rolling window method to make predictions
  • Attempted over-sampling techniques to deal with imbalanced dataset
  • Completed feature engineering by identifying correlation within variables
